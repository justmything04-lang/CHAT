📌 Attendance Automation & Parent Communication System

⸻

1. Introduction

Our product is a Telegram-based automated attendance & parent communication system, designed specifically for academies.
It reduces manual work, improves transparency, and keeps parents engaged in real time.

The system works batch-wise and subject-wise:
	•	Individual Plan: 1 Bot = 1 Batch + 1 Subject
	•	Group Plan: 1 Bot = 1 Batch + Up to 3 Subjects

This ensures accuracy, customization, and complete control for each group.

⸻

2. Key Problems We Solve

❌ Manual attendance tracking consumes faculty time.
❌ End-of-day absentee calculation and reporting is delayed.
❌ Parents are unaware about absences until much later.
❌ No structured performance reports (weekly, bi-weekly, monthly).
❌ Communication gaps between academy ↔ student ↔ parents.

✅ With our bot, all these problems are solved automatically.

⸻

3. Features (Complete Automation)

📍 Attendance Marking
	•	Students mark attendance with one tap (online mode) or with location proof (offline mode).
	•	Prevents proxies by verifying Telegram ID and GPS.
	•	Works only within academy’s allowed time window.

📊 End-of-Day (EOD) Reports
	•	Auto-generated absentee lists at the end of each day.
	•	Faculty gets both offline & online absentee reports.
	•	Parents of absentees notified automatically (Telegram).

📅 Bi-Weekly Reports (Every 14 days)
	•	Attendance summary of each student (present, absent, %).
	•	Categorization: 100%, High, Average, Low (<60%).
	•	Faculty/Admin get detailed sheet link + summary in group topic.
	•	Parents of “Low” students alerted with caution messages.
	•	Optionally, academy app can restrict access for Low-attendance cases.

📅 Monthly Reports
	•	Monthly summary with “Risk Watch” (≤50%).
	•	Parents of “Risk” students notified with absence dates.
	•	Faculty/Admin receive Google Sheet tab link + clean summary in group topic.

📘 Course Summary (Till Date)
	•	Complete overview from course start till current date.
	•	Risk tracking across full subject duration.
	•	New tab created in Google Sheet, shared with faculty/admin.

🏆 Top 3 Performers
	•	Automated ranking of top 3 attendance performers.
	•	Posted in group topic + sent to faculty.

👨‍👩‍👧 Parent Communication
	•	Parent accounts linked with student IDs.
	•	Parents receive instant absentee updates.
	•	Parents of Low/Risk attendance students alerted separately.
	•	WhatsApp invite (one-time) ensures smooth onboarding.

📱 Teacher/Admin Controls

Simple Telegram panel:
	•	📊 Top 3
	•	📅 EOD Report
	•	📅 Bi-Weekly Report
	•	📅 Monthly Report
	•	📘 Course Summary
	•	🔄 Refresh Attendance
	•	🕒 Change Timings

No need to check sheets daily — bot manages everything.

⸻

4. Ease of Use

👩‍🎓 Students → Just tap “Mark Attendance” once a day.
👨‍👩‍👧 Parents → Get auto Telegram updates, invited via WhatsApp (one-time).
👩‍🏫 Faculty → View all reports directly in Telegram & Sheets.
🏫 Management → Get real-time risk tracking & optional app restrictions.

Everything runs automatically in the background.
